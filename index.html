Rupamjyoti Ray

Rupamjyoti Ray, professionally known as Mr. RJR, is an Indian web developer, digital entrepreneur, and educational content creator based in Dhubri, Assam. He is the founder of Latest Online Apply and the Director of Skillup Computer Academy. Through his platforms, he provides government job notifications, admit cards, examination results, current affairs, online application guidance, and competitive examination resources for students and job seekers across India.

Early Life

Rupamjyoti Ray was born in Assam, India. He developed an early interest in computers, internet technologies, and digital media. His passion for learning web technologies led him to create online platforms aimed at making educational and career-related information more accessible.

Career

Ray established Latest Online Apply with the objective of helping students and job aspirants by providing accurate information about government recruitment, competitive examinations, scholarships, and online services. He also serves as the Director of Skillup Computer Academy, where he oversees computer literacy, certification programs, and full-stack development curricula.

In addition to managing educational platforms, he develops websites, WordPress solutions, PHP applications with MySQL database integrations, AI-powered web tools, and digital services focused on improving access to education through technology.

Vision

Rupamjyoti Ray aims to use technology to make educational resources, career guidance, and government services more accessible. Through innovation and user-friendly digital solutions, he continues to support students and job seekers across India.

Recognition

Rupamjyoti Ray is recognized for his contributions to educational technology and digital information services through the development of platforms that assist students, competitive examination aspirants, and job seekers with reliable online resources.
